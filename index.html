Socializing

Socializing refers to the act of interacting and engaging with others in a social context. It involves communication, whether verbal or non-verbal, to establish and maintain connections, share experiences, and build relationships. Socializing plays a vital role in human development, fostering a sense of belonging, empathy, and understanding within a community or society.


Flan Making

Leche flan making is a culinary process that involves creating a popular Filipino dessert
known for its creamy and caramelized texture. It begins by preparing a mixture of eggs,
condensed milk, and evaporated milk, which is sweetened and flavored with vanilla.
The mixture is then poured into a caramel-coated mold and steamed or baked until it sets,
resulting in a smooth custard dessert that's enjoyed for its rich and sweet taste.
